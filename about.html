Here are some projects I have been apart of.

Front End Developer

Ultimately, we agreed music was something we were all passionate about and something we would enjoy designing an app around. Due our experience at the time we were limited in the scope of our application, but the idea was still interesting to build around. I was tasked with designing and implementing the vast majority of the look and structure of the site itself. Check out my work page to view the website.

Grandma's Attic

Full Stack Developer

This application was designed around the idea that people have a lot of junk and it can be quite a hassle to get pack up and move all of this junk. Some people don't have the patience or time to nickel and dime people for old trinkets and household items. In comes Grandma's Attic. All users need do is input what kind of item they are trying to get rid of and their location, and someone can claim those items. My job was primarily centered around setting up the server to hold client's item information, as well as implementing the API (called Cloudinary) to store images in a separate database.

App-a-Day

Full Stack Developer

This application was a culmination of everything I and my classmates had learned over the course of our class. The idea behind it was simply that it's hard to remember all of this new information, we need ways to keep all this knowledge fresh. App-a-Day was our answer. Once users login, they are allowed to participate in a few Javascript oriented activites. The first are small step by step instructional javascript coding sections. The second activity is video page allowing for searches specific to javascript. I very much took both a leadership role this project. I designed the look and feel of the app. I built the foundation, and delegated activities to other team members to ensure there was nobody working on the same code. I implemented many of the technologies such as Redux, Express, Mongoose, React-Router, and ReactStrap. I managed the Github Repo, and I connected the app to Heroku.
